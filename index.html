Why this Blog?

Why this blog you ask? Well, I was just getting bored. And with nothing better to do than playing PUBG, I thought why not learn something instead of just playing games.

If I loved having my head glued to screen so much, wouldn't it be better to learn somthing instead. Since both require doing the same thing: staring at the screen. The only difference being that the later is profitable and the former, a time-pass.

So that's how it all stared. And here's the first project : A Blog!.

This is still a work-in-progress. But I hope to complete it soon. Okay, that's all for the intro.

Thank you for taking your time to read my post. I'd love to hear your thoughts on it and you know, improve it in if possible.
